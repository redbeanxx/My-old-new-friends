A normal day as usual, Girl took the elevator tiredly. From birthplace to big city, there is far away from home. In this indifferent city, she felt incompatible with the world.

She looked around the crowed people. There was one person look at her at first, then everyone look at her. Everyone surrounded her closer and closer. She thorught she is going to be swallowed by the fury people. The air compressed and couldn't breathe. She felt helpless and uneasy. Hoping someone will save her.

Suddenly, Hu ye appeared and the fury monster retreated fome the girl.  Hu ye fend the monster and saved the girl. However, the girl look the ferocious beast still felt afriad. She afraid to approach Hu ye.

 

Then, the button monster made the whole place flooding. The girl flooded and fall into memories.When she was little, she played in the hometown forest. Then there was many monsters appeared from the river. The monsters saw her and chased her. She had to keep running.

 

When she too tried to escape, the monsters almost catch her. In that time, Hu ye appeared. He drived away the monsters saved the little girl. She realized that Hu ye and she was already friends in the past.

 

In the elevater, the girl give Hu ye a big hug. The elevator arrived. She go out the elevator and Hu ye still protect her. No matter how far from the hometome, they always friends.
